Sign up for an emnify account

To begin developing your IoT solutions with emnify, you'll need an emnify account. Sign up for free to a 2-month trial to use all functionalities.

Need help verifying your phone number?

When filling out the sign-up form, you may have issues verifying your phone number.

Here are a few common error messages and what to do if you see them:

SMS verification not possible
  • Try registering with another phone number.
  • Contact the emnify sales team to set up your account.
You've reached the maximum login attempts
  • Wait at least 10 minutes, and then try to log in again.
  • If you've waited and your next attempt is blocked, contact the emnify sales team.
The verification code you entered is incorrect
  • Re-enter your code and click Verify again.
  • Click Resend Code and enter the new verification code once you've received it.
  • Still having trouble? Contact the emnify sales team.
The CAPTCHA you entered is incorrect
  • Re-enter the displayed CAPTCHA characters.
  • Keep in mind that CAPTCHA shows both upper and lowercase letters, so it's important to capitalize them exactly as they're shown.
  • Still having trouble? Contact the emnify sales team.

emnify's Evaluation Plan​

After signing up, your account is automatically assigned to the Evaluation Plan. With the Evaluation Plan, you have 60 days to use the issued prepaid credit for testing emnify's services.

Once this expires, you'll need to choose a new plan.

tip

Information on your current balance and other details about the Evaluation plan is available in the emnify Portal.
In the Organization Settings, select Subscription.

Supported features​

In addition to the standard emnify services, the Evaluation Plan supports the following features:

Global virtual private network

Establish secure private connections to your devices via a virtual private network (VPN), so you can troubleshoot devices.

Access to 500+ networks

Connect your IoT devices to more than 540 networks in over 180 countries. Get 2G, 3G, 4G, 5G, LTE-M, and NB-IoT coverage.

Multicloud Data Streamer

Use the tools you're familiar with to stream connectivity metadata, triage issues, collect usage insights, and more.Learn more about the Data Streamer or browse the available integrations.

Access via APIs, SDKs, Zapier

Automate SIM management, business processes, and other workflows using emnify's suite of developer tools.

  • APIs (REST and GraphQL)
  • SDKs (Python and Java)
  • Zapier (for no-code workflow automation)

Order a free Evaluation SIM package​

You can order your free Evaluation SIM package on the emnify Portal. This package includes an issued prepaid credit for testing emnify's services.

note

You have 60 days to use this prepaid credit.

Currently, the three triple-cut SIM cards can be delivered to most destinations worldwide with express shipment.

To get your free Evaluation SIM package, log in to your emnify account and follow these steps:

  1. On the Dashboard, click Get SIMs on the Get your free SIMs panel.
  1. Enter your Invoice data, Delivery details, and Choose shipping option.
  1. Finally, review and confirm your details.
info

You'll be notified when the SIM cards will be shipped.
