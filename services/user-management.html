User management

The emnify Portal is a powerful application to control the connectivity of devices of a production system.

Employees across your organization may use the Portal, from operations and finance to development and product. That's why emnify offers 3 levels of access (referred to as Roles) to use and manage Portal features and functionality:

  1. Admin (has access to all services and user management)
  2. Observer (has access to limited services)
  3. User (has access to limited services)

You can view and edit these roles on the Employees page under your Organization settings.
